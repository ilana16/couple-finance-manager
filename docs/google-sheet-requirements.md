# Google Sheet Budget Requirements

Based on the "Personal monthly budget current" Google Sheet, the following features are needed:

## Overview Tab Features

### Projected Section
1. **Projected Expenses** - Total projected monthly expenses ($5,238.00)
2. **Projected spending balance** - Projected income minus monthly and weekly expenses ($1,962.00)
3. **Projected Average Remaining Flexible Balance For The Month** ($1,962.00)
4. **Projected Weekly average remaining flexible balance** ($490.00)
5. **Projected Daily remaining average flexible balance** ($81.00)
6. **Projected income** ($7,200.00)

### Actual Section
7. **Actual Expenses** - Real-time tracking
8. **Actual balance** - Actual income minus actual expenses
9. **Average Remaining Flexible Balance For The Month** ($10,262.00)
10. **Weekly average remaining flexible balance** ($2,565.50)
11. **Daily remaining average flexible balance** ($427.58)
12. **Projected expenses still to be debited from my account** ($4,938.00)
13. **Projected payments still to be payed to me** ($7,200.00)
14. **Total available credit monthly** ($8,000.00)
15. **Current available credit remaining for the month** ($8,000.00)

### Savings Section
16. **Monthly Savings** - Track monthly savings amount

## Key Calculations Needed

1. **Flexible Balance Calculations**
   - Monthly average remaining
   - Weekly average remaining  
   - Daily average remaining

2. **Projected vs Actual Tracking**
   - Compare projected expenses with actual
   - Track spending balance (income - expenses)
   - Monitor credit availability

3. **Income & Expense Tracking**
   - Projected income
   - Actual income
   - Projected expenses
   - Actual expenses
   - Pending debits (expenses to be charged)
   - Pending credits (payments to receive)

4. **Credit Management**
   - Total available credit
   - Current available credit remaining
   - Credit utilization tracking

5. **Savings Tracking**
   - Monthly savings goals
   - Actual savings accumulated

## Additional Tabs
- **Budget Form Calculations** - Backend calculations
- **Ilana's Budget Form** - Individual budget entry form
- **Numbers** - Detailed transaction numbers

## Implementation Requirements

The couple finance app should include:

1. **Dashboard with Projected vs Actual**
   - Side-by-side comparison of projected and actual figures
   - Real-time balance calculations
   - Flexible spending balance (daily/weekly/monthly averages)

2. **Credit Card Management**
   - Track total credit limit
   - Monitor available credit
   - Credit utilization percentage

3. **Pending Transactions**
   - Expenses still to be debited
   - Payments still to be received
   - Impact on available balance

4. **Advanced Calculations**
   - Automatic calculation of flexible balance
   - Daily/weekly/monthly averages
   - Projected vs actual variance

5. **Individual Budget Forms**
   - Separate forms for each partner
   - Ability to input projected expenses
   - Track individual spending

6. **Savings Automation**
   - Automatic savings calculation
   - Monthly savings goals
   - Progress tracking
